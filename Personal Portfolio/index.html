About Me:

Originally from Iowa, I joined the Navy in 2012 and served for 10 years, and counting, as an Electricians Mate onboard submarines since 2014. I served for 6 years on the USS Alabama (Roll Tide) and am now on my shore duty. I will have 11 years of naval service under my belt at my End of Active Obligated Service (EAOS) April of 2023

I started my coding journey in 2020 while taking some collage classes while I was in the Navy. At the time I was persuing a degree in Electrical Engineering and one of the earlier required classes was an Intro to Programming course where we learned Java. That was my first introduction into coding and it was one of the only classes I've taken that I can honestly say I've been engrossed in and thouroghly enjoyed. I kept tinkering around with coding on and off since then but with no real focus and only with the Java language. This first week has proven to me that this course will be fast paced but I think it's going to be very enjoyable.
